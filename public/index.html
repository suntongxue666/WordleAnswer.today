How to Play Wordle

Wordle is a popular online word puzzle game. The objective is to guess a five-letter word in six tries. After each guess, the game provides feedback:

  • Green: Letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
  • Yellow: Letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • Gray: Letter is not in the word at all.

Use these clues to refine your next guess. Good luck!
